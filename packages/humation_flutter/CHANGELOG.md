## 0.1.0

- Initial release. The `HumationAvatar` widget backed by a native `CustomPainter`
  renderer for the supported SVG subset, live colour-slot recolouring, a parsed
  geometry cache, the `HumationPartPreview` thumbnail widget, the `Humation`
  facade, and PNG or image export. Typed slot constants (`HumationSlot`,
  `HumationColorSlot`) and colours that accept a Flutter `Color` or a hex string.
  Bundles the `humation-1` pack. Runs on iOS, Android, web, and desktop.
